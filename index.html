Task 3: Activation Ideation - London Outdoor Event

Objective: Propose an engaging, interactive booth activation for a large (5k attendees) outdoor event in London that aligns with CoderPad's brand and attracts the target audience.

Visual Concept

Interactive 'Code Breaker' puzzle challenge with physical lockboxes and live leaderboard display.

CoderPad "Code Breaker" Challenge 🕵️‍♀️

The Scenario:

At a bustling outdoor event in London with 5,000 attendees, standing out requires more than just a standard booth. We need an activation that draws people in, keeps them engaged, and creates memorable connections with CoderPad's brand values.

The Activation:

The "Code Breaker" challenge presents attendees with hands-on logic puzzles that simulate real-world coding problems. Participants have ~3 minutes to solve each puzzle, with a timer adding excitement. Successful solutions unlock a physical lockbox containing small prizes (like quality branded swag). A live leaderboard displays top performers, encouraging friendly competition.

Why it Works for CoderPad:

  • Focuses on Skills - Direct metaphor for CoderPad's value
  • Efficient & Engaging - Reflects the platform's nature
  • "Unlocking Potential" - Thematic tie-in
  • Natural Conversation Starter - Easily connects activity

Potential Benefits:

  • Increased Booth Traffic - Challenge draws crowds
  • Qualified Lead Generation - Attracts problem-solvers
  • Memorable Brand Experience - Positive association
  • Meaningful Engagement - Deeper conversations

Implementation Details:

Tech Setup

2-3 tablets/puzzle stations, screen for leaderboard, lockbox mechanism

Staffing

2-3 staff for rules, managing stations, and follow-up

Prizes

Quality branded swag, maybe raffle entry for larger prize
