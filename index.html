Booth Activation Idea

An interactive experience designed to draw crowds and engage the target audience at a large outdoor event in London (~5,000 attendees).

CoderPad "Code Breaker" Challenge üïµÔ∏è‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è

The Scenario:

Imagine the bustling energy of an outdoor London event. To cut through the noise and capture attention, we need an activation that embodies CoderPad's focus on skill and creates genuine interaction.

The Activation:

Attendees face a fun, hands-on challenge: solve logic puzzles on a tablet within ~3 minutes to reveal a combination, unlock a physical lockbox, and win cool branded swag. A live leaderboard fuels friendly competition.

Interactive Skill-Based Competitive Memorable

Key Components

  • Engaging logic puzzles (tablet/cards)
  • ~3-minute timer per attempt
  • Physical lockbox w/ combination lock
  • Quality branded swag prizes
  • Optional digital leaderboard
  • Clear CoderPad branding

Why It Works for CoderPad

  • Tangibly demonstrates focus on skills
  • Creates engaging, memorable interaction
  • Attracts relevant audience (problem-solvers)
  • Provides easy conversation starters for staff
  • Differentiates CoderPad's booth

Potential Outcomes

Deeper interactions, increased dwell time.
